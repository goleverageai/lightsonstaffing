Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you fill a position?

Our average time-to-fill is 2-3 weeks for most positions, though this can vary based on role complexity and requirements. For urgent needs, we can often provide qualified candidates within 48-72 hours.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have deep expertise in technology, healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and professional services. Our team's diverse background allows us to serve clients across multiple sectors effectively.

Do you offer temporary staffing?

Yes, we provide both temporary and permanent staffing solutions. Our flexible approach includes contract-to-hire options, project-based staffing, and seasonal workforce solutions.

What is your fee structure?

Our fees are competitive and transparent, typically based on a percentage of the placed candidate's annual salary. We offer different pricing models depending on your specific needs and volume requirements.

Do you provide candidate guarantees?

Yes, we stand behind our placements with comprehensive guarantees. If a placed candidate doesn't work out within the guarantee period, we'll provide a replacement at no additional cost.

How do you ensure candidate quality?

Our rigorous screening process includes skills assessments, background checks, reference verification, and cultural fit evaluation. We only present candidates who meet your specific requirements.
